Adventurers, it’s time for another <b>Ancient Dungeon</b> devlog discussing the next update that is rolling out on PSVR2, Quest, and PCVR! This one is packed with exciting new content, quality-of-life improvements, and of course, some classic bug fixes. <b>This update is scheduled for release at the end of this month.</b>

Let's dive into what is coming:

## New Additions

- <b>One New Orb</b>
- <b>11 New Relics</b> - more ways to customize your runs!
- <b>10 new Insight Upgrades</b> - Plus, a redesigned insight board to refine progression.

![](https://clan.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/images/35672451/3253fde453cb38e3baae08a5fab847364b5e14e6.jpg)

- <b>Exploring Points of Interest</b> - A new Layer of Adventure! This upcoming update brings Points of Interest (POIs)unique areas designed to add more variety, challenge, and intrigue to your dungeon runs!

<b>What are POIs?</b>
- These areas break up regular dungeon rooms with new enemies, semi-bosses, puzzles, or just better loot. Some are designed to test your skills, while others add fresh environmental storytelling to Ancient Dungeon.

![](https://clan.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/images/35672451/e4a636be781a3038c5c5e725253348486f888a98.jpg)

<u>Examples of POI</u>
<b>Lost Storage</b> – A massive, cluttered room filled with crates and lost relics. This acts as a more rewarding item room replacement. Who knows what treasures (or dangers) lie within?

![](https://clan.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/images/35672451/01cefcf4d779ddfdb739be5c2f9ca1f682a5c8b5.jpg)

<b>Entangled Thicket</b> – A twisting maze of vegetation and thorns. You’ll have to slash and carve your way through to escape! This replaces standard corridor and enemy rooms, adding a unique traversal challenge.

![](https://clan.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/images/35672451/cb7f5738e5e75eb4a4fc7816388744df1bcaae38.jpg)

These are just two examples, many more POI's await for you to discover!

[*] <b>New Ethereal Heart type</b>s – More ways to manage health!

![](https://clan.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/images/35672451/09cfce781fade9d787cc513af5f9e0ae162aca37.jpg)

[*] <b>New food types</b> (yes, including CHEESE) - MOR snacks, I mean who doesn't like to eat?
[*] <b>Autosaving during Dungeon Runs</b> - No more lost progress mid-run!
[*] <b>More Boss Room Layouts for The Beast's Cradle</b> - Greater variety and fresh challenges!
[*] <b>Enemy Bestiary</b> - Learn more about the creatures lurking in the depths.

![](https://clan.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/images/35672451/77524ad95941230ce562e95bd03eb8e629d98815.jpg)

[*] <b>Player Statistics</b> - Track your Dungeon runs and flex your adventuring skills.

![](https://clan.cloudflare.steamstatic.com/images/35672451/207ca55dbf479c03410ef79cf4aa297b554bd109.jpg)

As always, a host of bug fixes and optimizations to improve your dungeon delving experience are packed in this upcoming update as well.
