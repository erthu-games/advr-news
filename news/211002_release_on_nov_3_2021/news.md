After more than two years of development, Ancient Dungeon will release into early access on Nov 3, 2021. The first early access version will just be the start of many content updates to come!

So what can you expect?

- 5 unique dungeon floors each with their own bosses, monsters, sound tracks and unique gameplay mechanics
- More than 100 different powerups that you can collect
- 38 achievements to complete
- 18 different journal pages to collect which will give you hints on how the Ancient Dungeon came to be
- 15+ different permanent upgrades that will introduce new mechanics into the dungeon, or change how your weapons behave.
- A final boss fight
- Two different weapon combos to master
- And much more!
